✦ Join the Guild ✦

The Adventurer's Guild welcomes capable travelers, explorers, scholars, warriors, and problem-solvers from across the realm. Membership provides access to guild resources, contracts, training opportunities, and a community of fellow adventurers.

Before You Apply

New members begin at the Bronze Rank and may advance by completing contracts, demonstrating good judgment, and following the Guild Code of Conduct. Applicants should be prepared to provide accurate information and accept the responsibilities that come with guild membership.

Basic Membership Expectations

  • Follow guild rules and posted contract requirements.
  • Treat fellow adventurers and guild staff with respect.
  • Report discoveries, completed quests, and unusual dangers.
  • Protect civilians and avoid unnecessary harm.
