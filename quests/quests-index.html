✦ Quest Board ✦

The Quest Board contains contracts submitted by guild offices, local leaders, merchants, travelers, and communities throughout the realm. Select a rank below to view the assignments available to adventurers of that standing.

Bronze Rank Quests

Entry-level contracts intended for new guild members. These quests usually involve local problems, basic investigations, deliveries, and minor dangers.

Missing Herbalist

Reward: 20 gp

A local herbalist failed to return from gathering plants near the eastern woods. Find them and bring them home safely.

Reward: 15 gp

Reward: 12 gp

A family heirloom was lost near the riverbank during a recent storm. Search the area and return it to its owner.

Reward: 18 gp

Guard the Orchard

Reward: 14 gp

Medicine Delivery

Reward: 16 gp

Deliver a package of medicine to a small village before nightfall. The forest road has recently become unsafe.

The Noisy Well

Reward: 12 gp

Strange scratching noises have been heard beneath the town well. Investigate the source without contaminating the water.

Runaway Goats

Reward: 8 gp

Six goats escaped from a hillside farm and wandered toward the old ruins. Return as many as possible unharmed.

Lanterns on the Road

Reward: 20 gp

Travelers report strange lights following them along the northern road. Discover what is causing the lights.

Missing Fishing Nets

Reward: 10 gp

Several fishing nets have disappeared from the docks. Find the missing equipment and identify who took it.

Reward: 18 gp

Escort a guild scribe to a nearby monument so they can record its inscriptions and return safely.

Reward: 15 gp

Reward: 12 gp

Fallen branches and debris have blocked the stream powering the village mill. Clear the obstruction and check for danger.

The Stolen Sign

Reward: 7 gp

The painted sign from a popular tavern disappeared overnight. Recover it before the evening crowd arrives.

Bees in the Bell Tower

Reward: 15 gp

A swarm of unusually aggressive bees has built a hive inside the town bell tower. Remove or relocate them safely.

Tracks Near the Schoolhouse

Reward: 20 gp

Large animal tracks have appeared near a rural schoolhouse. Identify the creature and prevent it from returning.

Recover the Courier Bag

Reward: 18 gp

A courier lost a leather message bag while crossing a narrow forest trail. Recover it before its contents are found.

The Flooded Shrine

Reward: 16 gp

Heavy rain has flooded a small roadside shrine. Retrieve the sacred items before the water rises further.

Night Watch at Briar Farm

Reward: 22 gp

Livestock have been disappearing from Briar Farm after sunset. Stand watch, discover the cause, and protect the remaining animals.
